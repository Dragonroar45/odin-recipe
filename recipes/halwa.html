Gajar ka Halwa

Gajar ka halwa, also known as Gajorer halwa, Gajarno halwo, Gajrela, Gajar pak, and carrot halwa Gajar ka halwa, also known as Gajorer halwa, Gajarno halwo, Gajrela, Gajar pak, and carrot halwa. The nuts and other items used are first sautéed in ghee, a type of clarified butter from the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 2 pounds carrots, grated
  • 1 ½ quarts whole milk
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ½ cup raisins or sultanas
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 8 tablespoons chopped pistachio nuts
